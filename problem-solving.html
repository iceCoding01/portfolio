Understand & Define

  • Problem statement definition and scope clarification
  • Success metrics and acceptance criteria establishment
2

Research & Analyze

Investigate existing solutions, analyze patterns, and identify root causes.

Research Methods:

Assess results, gather feedback, and continuously optimize the solution.
